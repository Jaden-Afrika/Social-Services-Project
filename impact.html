About Umoja Plates

Since 2015, we've been working tirelessly to ensure no one in our community goes hungry. Through partnerships with local businesses, farms, and dedicated volunteers, we've created a sustainable food donation network that serves thousands of families every month.

Our Mission

To eliminate hunger in our community by connecting food donors with those in need through efficient distribution and compassionate service.

Our Vision

A community where no one goes hungry and everyone has access to nutritious, fresh food regardless of their circumstances.

Our Story

FoodShare began in 2015 when a group of community members noticed the growing gap between food waste and food insecurity. While restaurants and grocery stores were discarding perfectly good food, many families in our community were struggling to put meals on the table. What started as a small weekend initiative has grown into a comprehensive food donation network serving over 15,000 families monthly.

We work with over 100 local businesses, farms, and food suppliers who donate excess food that would otherwise go to waste. Today, we operate distribution centers across the city, run mobile food pantries in underserved neighborhoods, and coordinate with partner organizations to ensure food reaches those who need it most. Our volunteer network has grown to over 500 dedicated individuals who share our vision of ending hunger in our community.
